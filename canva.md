# Canva Pro Access

Global AI provides **Canva Pro access** to eligible and active chapter leads to help create branded event graphics and promotional content.

This benefit is available to chapters that are actively organizing and publishing Global AI events.


## Who Is Eligible

You are eligible for Canva Pro access if:

- You are an active Global AI Chapter Lead
- You are organizing Global AI events
- Your events are published on the Global AI website
- You have at least one **upcoming chapter event scheduled** and not AgentCamp
- You actively promote events using Global AI branding

Canva Pro access is reviewed and approved by Global AI HQ.


## How to Request Canva Pro Access

All requests must be sent from your **@globalai.community** chapter email.

**Email**: [support@globalai.community](mailto:support@globalai.community)

Your request must include details of your upcoming Global AI event.

Requests sent from personal emails will not be considered.

## Important Notes

- Canva Pro access is provided for **chapter use only**
- Access may be revoked if chapters become inactive
- All designs must follow Global AI branding guidelines
