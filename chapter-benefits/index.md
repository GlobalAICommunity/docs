# Chapter Benefits

Your Global AI chapter has access to a range of member benefits designed to support event delivery, community growth, and chapter visibility.

Below is a quick guide to all chapter benefits, with links to the dedicated page where you can find full details and request instructions.

## Chapter Benefits Overview

- [Azure Subscriptions](azure.md)
  - Sponsor credits for workshops and attendee hands-on labs.
  - Includes guidance on request requirements and activation.

- [Azure Subscription Activation Guide](azure-client.md)
  - Step-by-step walkthrough for turning sponsorship approval into a usable Azure subscription.

- [Digital Badges](badges.md)
  - Award event attendees with verified Global AI digital badges.
  - Learn how to enable badges from your Chapter Space event settings.

- [Canva Pro Access](canva.md)
  - Request Canva Pro for branded event graphics and promotional materials.
  - Available to eligible active chapter leads with published Global AI events.

- [Discord Access](discord.md)
  - Join the official Global AI Discord server and request the Chapter Lead role.
  - Stay connected with HQ and the global chapter lead community.

- [LinkedIn Showcase Page](linkedin-showcase-page.md)
  - Request a LinkedIn Showcase Page for your chapter to promote events and updates.
  - Includes email requirements and chapter lead profile details.

- [MeetUp Sponsorship](meetup.md)
  - Apply for access to the Global AI MeetUp community and create or connect your chapter group.
  - Sponsorship is earned through active chapter engagement and branding compliance.

## How to use this page

Visit the dedicated page for each benefit to find:

- eligibility requirements
- request instructions
- email templates and subject lines
- step-by-step onboarding guidance

If your chapter is new, start with the benefits that support visibility and community growth: Discord access and LinkedIn Showcase Page. Once your events are running, use Azure sponsorship and MeetUp sponsorship to expand your chapter offerings.
