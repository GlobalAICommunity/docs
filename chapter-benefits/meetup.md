# MeetUp

*Posted: 16 June 2026*

**An important update about how Global AI Community chapters are organized online.**

After 7 years of supporting [Meetup.com](https://www.meetup.com/pro/the-global-ai-community/), we will no longer be paying for the platform on behalf of our chapters.

The decision was not taken lightly, but the numbers are clear:

- Over **$20,000 a year** in costs
- More than **half of chapters** not meeting our community guidelines
- **No real growth** to show for it

## A purpose-built home for Global AI

The good news: our own platform is ready, and it gives you more than Meetup ever did. It's purpose-built for our community, and we're confident it's the right home for Global AI going forward.

## What happens next

The cutover will happen somewhere in **August or September 2026**. We'll reach out to chapter organizers directly with next steps before then.

If you have questions in the meantime, feel free to [get in touch](mailto:support@globalai.community).
