# February 2026

**Global AI Community: February 2026 Update & New Resources**

February has been a month of incredible momentum for the Global AI Community. We are continuing our 2026 roadmap with significant updates to our event infrastructure, the launch of a new governance model, and major enhancements to our digital tools.

## 1. AgentCon & AgentCamp Success
The year is off to an explosive start with events already spanning the globe.
* **Early Momentum**: We have already successfully hosted five [AgentCon](https://agentcon.city) events in Vienna, Istanbul, Oslo, Stockholm, and Zurich.
* **Upcoming Schedule**: There are at least 15 more AgentCons scheduled globally, including an upcoming event in Bangkok.
* **Speaker Opportunities**: Call for Speakers is currently open for several regions; you can apply directly at agentcon.city.
* **AgentCamp Reach**: [AgentCamp](https://agentcamp.city) is now active in 63 locations across 24 countries.
* **New Resources**: We have released a brand-new, 15-minute [keynote video](/agentcamp/content.md) for AgentCamp.

## 2. Community Governance & Advisory Board
We are introducing new ways to lead and support our global chapters.
* **Advisory Board Launch**: We are in the early stages of launching the Global AI Advisory Board to help guide the community's future.
* **Selection Process**: The inaugural board will consist of five members selected by the Global AI team, with a voting system to be implemented for future tenures.
* **Call for Suggestions**: If you know a community leader who would be a good fit, please share their LinkedIn profile in the [#ideas-and-suggestions](https://gaic.io/discord) channel on Discord.
* **Meetup Support**: We are improving our meetup sponsorship guidelines and will be reaching out to inactive chapters to offer help and resources to restart local activities.


## 3. Platform Enhancements & Tools
We are building a more integrated experience for chapter leads and attendees.
* **Integrated Registration**: You can now manage event registrations directly on the Global AI platform, including setting attendee limits.
* **Direct Communication**: Organizers can now send  email updates to their attendees directly through the Global AI mail server to ensure higher deliverability.
* **Platform Revamp**: Work is underway on a new platform featuring public profiles where members can showcase the events they have organized, attended, and the badges they have earned.


## 4. Discord & Documentation
Our community hub is growing rapidly as we finalize our move away from older platforms.
* **Member Milestone**: The Global AI Discord has officially surpassed 500 members. [Join Now](https://gaic.io/discord).
* **Localized Channels**: We are creating dedicated country-specific channels (e.g., India, South Korea, Thailand) to help leads engage with their local members.
* **Meeting Archives**: A [dedicated recap page](/chapter-leads-meeting/index.md) is now live where you can find all past meeting recordings and summaries.
