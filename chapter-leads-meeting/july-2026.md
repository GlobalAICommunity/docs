# July 2026

**Global AI Community: July 2026 Update & New Resources**

July has been a season of wrap-ups and a fresh start. AgentCamp and Build // localhost both closed out strong seasons, our community keeps growing across every channel, and we're consolidating chapter tools into one new Global AI platform. Here's a full recap of what we covered.


## 1. AgentCamp & Build // localhost: That's a Wrap

Both flagship programs closed out their 2026 seasons with record reach.

- **AgentCamp 2026**: Wrapped with **105 locations** across **35 countries**, delivering **420+ hours** of AI content.
- **Build // localhost**: Wrapped with **60 locations** across **35 countries**, delivering **240 hours** of AI content, run in partnership with Microsoft Build.

A huge ***thank you*** to every chapter lead, organizer, speaker, and attendee who made this season possible.

## 2. Community Growth

The community keeps growing across every channel.

- **Chapter Network**: We're now at **200 chapters** worldwide.
- **YouTube**: Our channel has crossed **50,000 subscribers**. [Subscribe here](https://www.youtube.com/@GlobalAICommunity).
- **Newsletter**: We've published **150+ issues** of Global AI Weekly.

## 3. Platform Migration: Meetup.com is Going Away

We're consolidating chapter tools into the new Global AI platform, and Meetup.com will no longer be part of that toolkit.

- **What's Changing**: Chapter events move fully to the new Global AI platform, and Meetup.com groups will be phased out.
- **Deadline**: **July 20, 2026** is the last day to claim and keep your `city_name@globalai.community` email address. Chapter leads should act before this date to avoid losing their chapter email.

## 4. Introducing the New Global AI Platform

Everything a chapter lead needs now lives in one place at [globalai.community/my](https://globalai.community/my).

- Manage your entire chapter from the website
- Send email to attendees
- Sell and manage tickets for attendees
- Scanner for the check-in process
- Sessions support to display speakers and agenda
- Upload post-event photos and a video link to the event page
- Automated review email sent after each event
- Login on up to 5 devices
- One inbox for notifications, badges, chapters, and your profile

## 5. New Event Series: Global AI Construct

We're launching a new hands-on, no-slides build format running July through September.

- **Format**: Chapters host sessions across no-code, low-code, and pro-code tracks.
- **For Chapter Leads**: Bring the format to your chapter with sessions tagged by skill level for easy planning.
- **Curriculum**: A full curriculum and session catalog is provided by HQ.
