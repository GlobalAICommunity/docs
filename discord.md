# Global AI Discord Access

As a **Global AI Chapter Lead**, you get access to the official Global AI Discord server.

This is the primary space to connect directly with **Global AI HQ** and chapter leads from across the world.

## Chapter Lead Role (Badge)

We have a dedicated **Chapter Lead role** on Discord.

This role:
- Makes you stand out from other community members
- Identifies you as an official Global AI Chapter Lead
- Gives better visibility in chapter related discussions

## How to Get the Chapter Lead Role

**Step 1: Join Discord**
- Join the Global AI Discord server: [discord.gg/NMryZKPC3m](https://discord.gg/NMryZKPC3m)
- Use your real name or chapter related username if possible

**Step 2: Email Support**
After joining Discord, send an email using your **@globalai.community** chapter email.

- **To**: support@globalai.community  
- **Subject**: Discord Chapter Lead Role Request  
- **Email content**:
  - Chapter name (Global AI CityName)
  - Your Discord username (including the # number)

Once verified, the Chapter Lead role will be assigned.


## Required Channel for Chapter Leads

All new chapter leads are encouraged to join and follow the:
`**#new-chapter-lead**` channel

This is where Global AI HQ shares updates, guidance, and announcements.

[Read more about chapter onboarding here](/chapter-onboarding.html#join-discord) for additional setup steps.
