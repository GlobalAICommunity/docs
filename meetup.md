# MeetUp

**As part of your chapter benefits you will get the opportunity to join the [Global AI Community MeetUp community](https://www.meetup.com/pro/the-global-ai-community/).**

This benefit is **not automatic** and must be requested and approved by Global AI HQ.

## Eligibility Requirements

Before requesting Meetup sponsorship, your chapter **must meet all of the following conditions**:

- Your Global AI chapter is officially created and approved
- You have completed the **New Chapter Onboarding Call**
- You have hosted **at least 1 in-person meetup within the first 3 months**
- You are an **active member in the Global AI Discord**  
  (especially the `#new-chapter-lead` channel)
- Your chapter **LinkedIn Showcase Page is live and active**
- Your chapter follows **Global AI branding and naming guidelines**


## Meetup Naming & Branding

To avoid common issues and ensure consistency across all chapters, please adhere to the following guidelines:

### Meetup Group Name
- Must be exactly: **Global AI CityName**
- No variations, emojis, or extra words

### Meetup Account
- Must be created using your **@globalai.community** email
- Account name must clearly represent the chapter or organizer

### Branding
- Only Global AI approved logos and banners are allowed
- Custom, unbranded, or outdated graphics are not permitted
- Branding must be downloaded from your Chapter Space on the Global AI website and [Branding Guidelines](/branding/index.md)

## How to Request Meetup Sponsorship

All Meetup sponsorship requests must be sent to: [support@globalai.community](mailto:support@globalai.community) using your chapter's official @globalai.community email address.

### Your email must include:

- Link to your LinkedIn Showcase Page
- Confirmation of at least 1 completed in-person event
- Meetup option you are requesting:
  - New Meetup group  
  - Connect Existing Meetup group

## Ongoing Expectations

Meetup sponsorship is maintained only if:

- Chapters continue hosting Global AI events
- Branding and naming remain compliant
- The chapter stays active on Discord
- LinkedIn page remains updated
- Events are aligned with Global AI values and goals
- Chapter must organise atleast 1 in-person event every 3 months

Global AI HQ reserves the right to review or revoke sponsorship if expectations are not met.

## Summary

- Meetup sponsorship is **earned**, not automatic
- All requests go through **support@globalaicommunity**
- Branding, naming, and activity standards are mandatory
- This process helps maintain a consistent and high-quality Global AI community worldwide
