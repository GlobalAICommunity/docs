# Global AI Community - My Community: User Guide

> **URL:** https://globalai.community/my  
> **Access:** Requires a registered and logged-in account.


## Overview

The **My Community** portal is your personal dashboard on the Global AI Community platform. It brings together all of your community activity in one place - including your event tickets, earned badges, events you manage, chapters you belong to, your profile, messages, and notification settings.


## How to Access My Community

1. Go to [globalai.community](https://globalai.community) and log in to your account.
2. Once logged in, click on your **profile avatar** in the top-right corner of the navigation bar.
3. A dropdown menu will appear - click **My Community** to enter the portal.

You can also navigate directly to `https://globalai.community/my` once logged in.

| Icon | Tab | URL |

The active section is highlighted in **orange**. You can click any tab to jump directly to that section at any time.


## 1. Home

**URL:** `https://globalai.community/my`

The home page is your community activity overview. It currently displays a welcome message:

> *"Welcome to My Community. This space is coming soon — here you will find an overview of your community activity."*

This section is being developed and will eventually show a summary of your tickets, badges, events, and chapter activity in one place.


## 2. Tickets

**URL:** `https://globalai.community/my/tickets`

The Tickets page shows all events you have registered for (i.e., your RSVP / ticket history).

### Layout

Tickets are divided into two sections:

**Upcoming** — Events you are registered for that have not yet taken place. Each card displays:
- Event banner image
- Event title
- Date
- Time and timezone
- Location (city/country for in-person events)

**Past** — Events you previously attended or registered for. Cards display the same details as upcoming events.

### How to use

- Browse your upcoming events to plan attendance.
- Scroll down to review your past event history.
- Click on any event card to view full event details.

> **Note:** Tickets are created automatically when you register for an event on the Global AI Community platform. You do not need to manually add tickets here.


## 3. Badges

**URL:** `https://globalai.community/my/badges`

The Badges page showcases all digital achievement badges you have earned through participation in the Global AI Community.

### Layout

The page displays:
- A heading: **"Your badges"**
- A subtitle: *"Badges you've earned for your achievements and participation in the Global AI Community."*
- A grid of badge cards, each showing:
  - Badge image/icon
  - Badge name (e.g., "AgentCamp - Attendee")
  - Date awarded
  - A **"View badge"** button (opens the badge credential in a new tab)

### How to use

- Click **View badge** on any badge card to open the full digital credential (hosted externally, e.g., on a credentialing platform).
- Share your badge link directly from the external credential page.
- Badges are awarded automatically when you attend qualifying events or reach milestones.


## 4. Events

**URL:** `https://globalai.community/my/events`

The Events page is for **event organizers and chapter leads** — it shows events you create or manage, not events you attend as a regular member (those are in Tickets).

### Layout

- **Description:** *"Events you organize or manage as a chapter lead."*
- **+ Add event** button (top right, orange) — for creating a new event.
- **Upcoming** section — upcoming events you are organizing.
- **Past** section — past events you have organized.

Each event card displays:
- Date (large day/month/year)
- Status badge (e.g., **Pending** in yellow)
- Event series/type label (e.g., **Global AI Bootcamp - New**, **Build //localhost**)
- Event title
- Time and timezone
- Location
- Associated chapter name

### Adding a New Event

Click **+ Add event** to open a 6-step event creation wizard:

| Step | Label | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | **Type** | Choose event type: **General event** (add to your chapter calendar) or join a **Global Event** series (e.g., Build //localhost) |
| 2 | **Registration** | Configure registration settings |
| 3 | **When** | Set the date and time |
| 4 | **Where** | Set the location |
| 5 | **Details** | Add title, description, and other details |
| 6 | **Confirm** | Review and submit the event |

> **Note:** Only users who are Chapter Leads can add events. Events submitted are reviewed and may show a **Pending** status until approved.

Learn more about event creation and management in the [Events Guide](/my-community/events-guide.md).

## 5. Chapters

**URL:** `https://globalai.community/my/chapters`

The Chapters page shows all the Global AI Community local chapters you are a member of or lead.

### Layout

- **Description:** *"Chapters are local groups that organize events and activities in your area."*
- **Start a chapter** button (top right, orange) — links to an external page to apply to start a new chapter.
- A grid of chapter cards.

Each chapter card displays:
- Chapter logo/badge image
- Chapter name (e.g., "Global AI Delhi")
- Your role badge: **Chapter Lead** (orange) or **Member** (grey)
- Stats:
  - Number of Leads
  - Number of Members
  - Upcoming events count
  - Past events count
  - Awards count

### Roles

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Chapter Lead** | You manage this chapter and can create/manage events for it |
| **Member** | You are a member of this chapter |

### Starting a Chapter

Click **Start a chapter** to open the application process on the Global AI Community website. This allows you to establish a new local chapter in your city or region.

## 6. Profile

**URL:** `https://globalai.community/my/profile`

The Profile page lets you manage your personal information displayed within the community.

### Editable Fields

**Personal Information:**
- Profile photo (click the × on the avatar to remove; click the avatar area to upload a new photo)
- First Name *(required)*
- Last Name *(required)*
- Country *(required)* — dropdown selector
- City *(required)*
- Company or School
- Job title or education
- Mobile phone — country code + number
- Birth Date *(required)* — year, month, day dropdowns

**Social Links:**
- GitHub — `https://github.com/username`
- LinkedIn — `https://www.linkedin.com/in/username`
- **+ Add social link** — dropdown to add more platforms

**Account:**
- Email *(required)* — shown but may not be editable directly here

### Saving Changes

After making any changes, click the orange **Save** button at the bottom of the page. Changes are saved immediately.

> **Tip:** Keep your profile up to date so other community members and chapter leads can connect with you accurately.

## 7. Messages (Mailbox)

**URL:** `https://globalai.community/my/mailbox`

The Messages / Mailbox page is a record of all emails the Global AI Community platform has sent to you.

### Layout

- **Heading:** "Mailbox"
- **Description:** *"All emails we have sent you."*
- If no messages have been received: *"You have no messages yet."*
- If messages exist: they appear as a list of email records.

### Purpose

This is a read-only archive — it stores system-generated emails such as:
- Event registration confirmations
- Badge award notifications
- Chapter updates
- Community announcements

> **Note:** You cannot send messages to other users from this page. It is an inbox for platform-generated emails only.

## 8. Settings

**URL:** `https://globalai.community/my/notifications`

The Settings page (accessible via the ⚙️ gear icon) controls your **email notification preferences**.

### Description

*"Choose which emails you want to receive from the Global AI Community."*

### Email Subscription Options

| Newsletter | Description | Default |
|------------|-------------|---------|
| **Global AI Weekly** | A weekly curated publication with interesting, relevant links | ✅ Subscribed |
| **Global AI Community Newsletter** | Information about the Global AI Community including global events | ✅ Subscribed |
| **Global AI Organizers Newsletter** | News and updates relevant to event organizers | ☐ Unsubscribed |
| **Global AI Chapter Lead Newsletter** | News and updates relevant to chapter leads | ☐ Unsubscribed |

### How to use

1. Check or uncheck the boxes next to each newsletter type.
2. Click the orange **Save** button to apply your preferences.

> **Tip:** If you are a Chapter Lead or Event Organizer, consider subscribing to the relevant newsletters to stay informed about important updates.

> **Note:** Site administrators will also see **Admin** and **Statistics** links in this dropdown. These are not visible to regular community members.
