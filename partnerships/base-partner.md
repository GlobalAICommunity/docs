# Base Partner

## Purpose

Base Partner status is intended for organizations that wish to actively support the Global AI Community through a modest annual contribution and closer engagement, without entering into commercial sponsorship.

## Eligibility

Organizations may qualify as Base Partners if they:

- Meet all Community Friend eligibility criteria
- Commit to an annual contribution as defined by GAC
- Demonstrate alignment with GAC’s mission and non-profit status
- Respect the independence of chapters and community activities

## Benefits
- Logo placement on the Global AI Community partner page
- Recognition as a “Base Partner of the Global AI Community”
- Access to partner briefings and selected community updates
- Introductory access to chapter leads upon mutual interest

## Limitations
- Do not receive guaranteed visibility, speaking slots, or recruitment promotion
- May not use Global AI Branding branding beyond approved partner references
- Do not receive member data or lead information
- Have no governance or decision-making authority
