# Community Partner

## Purpose

The Community Partner level exists to recognize organizations that support the mission and values of the Global AI Community and wish to stay connected to the ecosystem in a non-commercial manner.

## Eligibility

Organizations may qualify as Community Partners if they:

- Are an established company, NGO, academic institution, public body, community, user group, conference, or initiative
- Support ethical, inclusive, and responsible AI development
- Agree to the Global AI Community Code of Conduct and values
- Do not seek commercial promotion or preferential treatment

## Benefits

- Recognition by name on the Global AI Community website
- Access to public partner communications and updates
- Invitations to public events and open initiatives

## Limitations

- Receive no commercial benefits
- Are not permitted to use the GAC logo
- May identify themselves only as a “Community Partner of the Global AI Community”
- Have no influence over community governance, content, or chapter activities

## Pricing

Community Partner status is **free**. Voluntary donations are appreciated and help support community activities, but do not provide additional commercial benefits.

## How to Become a Community Partner

Contact **[hq@globalai.community](mailto:hq@globalai.community)**. Applications are reviewed for alignment with our mission, values, and community-first approach.
