# Global AI Weekly

[Global AI Weekly](https://weekly.globalai.community/) is our curated weekly publication for AI developers, data scientists, students, professionals, and community leaders. It reaches **70,000+ subscribers** with useful AI news, resources, events, and learning opportunities.

## Partner Opportunities

Depending on the partnership agreement, approved newsletter opportunities may include:

- Clearly identified partner content
- Product credits, licenses, subscriptions, or community offers
- Educational resources, technical articles, or learning materials
- Relevant event, workshop, or program announcements

All newsletter content is subject to editorial review and must provide clear value to readers. A newsletter placement does not imply endorsement by the Global AI Community.

## Privacy

Partners do not receive subscriber email addresses, personal data, or direct access to the mailing list. The Global AI Community manages all newsletter distribution.
