# Global AI Community Sponsorship

**This page is for organisations that want to partner with the Global AI Community.**

The **Global AI Community** is an international not-for-profit network of AI enthusiasts and professionals, uniting over **175 chapters worldwide** and reaching **250,000+ members**. Our chapters host local meetups, global conferences, and produce online content (YouTube series, interviews) to share AI knowledge. Sponsorships enable us to sustain and grow these initiatives. By partnering with the Global AI Community, your organization gains visibility and engagement within a vibrant worldwide AI network while directly supporting AI education, diversity, and innovation at a grassroots level.



## Partnership Levels

### Community Partner
Free, non-commercial recognition for organizations that support the mission of the Global AI Community and want to stay connected.
[Read more](./community-partner.md)

### Partner
A low-fee partnership for organizations that want to actively support the Global AI Community and  seeking brand visibility, engagement, and sponsorship opportunities across chapters, events, and content.

### Strategic Partner
A long-term collaboration focused on shared goals, impact, and sustained engagement with the Global AI ecosystem.

## Why partner with the Global AI Community

-  **Global Reach**: Tap into a community of 150+ chapters across continents, connecting with **250k+ AI developers, data scientists, students, and professionals**. Sponsorship offers unparalleled exposure to a diverse, worldwide audience in the AI field.
-  **Active Events Calendar**: Chapters host frequent in-person and virtual events. In addition, we organize flagship **Global AI events** and conferences. Sponsors are highlighted at these gatherings, benefiting from on-site branding and live recognition.
- **Online & Year-Round Presence**: Our **online channels** – including YouTube series, webinars, podcasts, and newsletters – keep the community engaged beyond physical events. Sponsors receive visibility in these digital contents, extending reach to community members who consume our videos and articles (total audience well beyond event attendees).
- **Community Support**: Your sponsorship (primarily through financial contributions) directly **funds local chapter activities, educational workshops, global conferences, and content creation**. It helps provide resources like training materials, venue rentals, and technology tools that empower our volunteer chapter leads and members.
- **In-Kind Contributions Welcomed**: We gladly accept in-kind support alongside or in lieu of financial funding. **Examples**: offering cloud service credits for hackathons, providing **speaker access** (notable experts from your team to present at meetups), or donating software/licensing for community use. These contributions are valuable to our community and count toward sponsorship status.
- **Mutual Benefit**: Sponsoring the Global AI Community is a **partnership**. Your organization gains **brand visibility**, talent engagement, and thought leadership opportunities, while the community gains essential support to continue its mission. Sponsors demonstrate leadership in AI and a commitment to fostering learning and collaboration on a global scale.
