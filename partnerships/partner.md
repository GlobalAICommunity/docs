# Partner

## Purpose

Partner status enables organizations to commercially support Global AI Community activities while gaining visibility and engagement opportunities aligned with specific events, chapters, or initiatives.

## Eligibility
Organizations may qualify as partner if they:

- Meet Base Partner eligibility criteria
- Commit to an annual contribution as defined by GAC
- Demonstrate alignment with GACâ€™s mission and non-profit status
- Respect the independence of chapters and community activities
- Enter into a written sponsorship agreement
- Operate in a manner consistent with ethical and responsible AI practices
- Agree to all sponsorship guidelines and brand usage policies

## Benefits
- Enhanced brand visibility according to the sponsorship agreement
- Sponsored presence at selected events or content initiatives
- Speaking or content opportunities (proposal-based)
- Optional recruitment or promotional opportunities where appropriate

## Limitations
- Do not influence editorial independence or chapter governance
- Receive no exclusivity unless explicitly agreed in writing
- Must respect local chapter autonomy and approval processes
- May not access or exploit community member data
