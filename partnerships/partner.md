# Partner

## Purpose

Partner status enables organizations to financially or materially support Global AI Community activities while receiving agreed visibility and engagement opportunities across specific events, chapters, or initiatives.

## Eligibility

Organizations may qualify as Partners if they:

- Are a legal organization
- Support ethical, inclusive, and responsible AI development
- Agree to the Global AI Community Code of Conduct and values
- Commit to the annual €6,000 partnership fee
- Offer any agreed in-kind contribution, such as product credits, licenses, subscriptions, tools, or training resources
- Enter into a written sponsorship agreement
- Agree to all sponsorship guidelines and brand usage policies
- Respect the independence of chapters and community activities

## Benefits

- Logo placement and recognition on the Global AI Community partner page
- Agreed visibility through relevant channels, including the community platform, Global AI Weekly, YouTube, and events
- Sponsored presence at selected events or content initiatives
- Access to Global AI Community event organizing tools for agreed event series
- Opportunities to offer approved products, credits, licenses, or subscriptions to relevant community audiences
- Speaking or content opportunities (proposal-based)
- Optional recruitment or promotional opportunities where appropriate

## Limitations

- Do not influence editorial independence or chapter governance
- Receive no exclusivity unless explicitly agreed in writing
- Must respect local chapter autonomy and approval processes
- May not claim that a product offer is endorsed by the Global AI Community
- May not access or exploit community member data

## Pricing

Partner status costs **€6,000 per year**.

## How to Become a Partner

Contact **[hq@globalai.community](mailto:hq@globalai.community)**. We will review your goals and proposed contribution before drafting a written partnership agreement.
