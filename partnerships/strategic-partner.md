# Strategic Partner

## Purpose

Strategic Partnerships are long-term collaborations with organizations that share strategic goals with the Global AI Community and contribute significantly to its mission and sustainability.

## Eligibility

Organizations may qualify as Strategic Partners if they:

- Demonstrate a long-term commitment to the Global AI Community mission
- Enter into a custom, written (multi-)year agreement
- Support ethical, inclusive, and responsible AI development
- Agree to the Global AI Community Code of Conduct and values
- Are approved by Global AI Community leadership

## Benefits

- Prominent partner recognition and co-branding (as agreed)
- Strategic collaboration on programs, content, or initiatives
- Access to dedicated coordination and advisory discussions
- Participation in agreed advisory or steering activities (non-binding)

## Limitations

- Do not control community governance or chapter operations
- Do not override community codes, policies, or independence
- Are subject to periodic review of alignment and impact

## Pricing

Strategic Partner pricing is defined in a custom multi-year agreement.

## How to Become a Strategic Partner

Contact **[hq@globalai.community](mailto:hq@globalai.community)** with your proposed strategic goals, contribution, and intended impact. Strategic Partnerships require leadership approval and a custom written agreement.
